The Forty Rules of Love, by Elif Shafak, 2009

NOVEL

Description:

The Forty Rules of Love is a novel written by the Turkish author Elif Shafak, The book was published in March 2009. It is about Maulana Jalal-Ud-Din, known as Rumi and his companion Shams Tabrizi. This book explains how Shams transformed a scholar into a Sufi (mystic) through love.

About the Author:

Elif ShafakFRSL is a Turkish-British novelist, essayist, public speaker, political scientist and activist. Shafak writes in Turkish and English, and has published 19 works.
